Our story

Started on a porch.
Still sounds
like one.

Front Porch Radio began in a back-shed studio with two mics, a turntable, and a stubborn belief that the radio — real radio, with a real person on the other end — still matters.

"Community radio is a front porch. You don't need an invitation — just pull up a chair."
