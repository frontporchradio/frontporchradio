Our Story

Started on a porch. Still sounds like one.

Front Porch Radio began in a back-shed studio with two mics, a turntable, and a stubborn belief that the radio — real radio, with a real person on the other end — still matters. We play the music that doesn't make the algorithm, and we pay attention to the artists who do the hard work of making it.

"Community radio is a front porch. You don't need an invitation — just pull up a chair."

We're independent, listener-supported, and commercial-free. Every dollar keeps the lights on, the records spinning, and the DJs paid.
